My Story


From the disciplined ranks of the Marine Corps to the creative studios of voice acting, my journey has been anything but ordinary. As a former radio operator, I honed the art of communication under the most challenging conditions, learning to convey messages with clarity, conviction, and a touch of humanity.


The transition from military service to the microphone was fueled by a passion for storytelling and a desire to bring characters to life. The skills I developed in the service—precision, reliability, and adaptability—have become the foundation of my voice acting career.


I’ve had the privilege of consulting with seasoned professionals in the industry, including voices that have echoed through the realms of iconic video games like Halo. Their mentorship has been invaluable, guiding me as I craft a vocal presence that is both versatile and engaging.


As I embark on this new chapter, I am eager to lend my voice to projects that inspire, entertain, and resonate. Let’s tell a story together.
